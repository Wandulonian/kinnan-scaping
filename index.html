June 18

The Driveway Was Poured — Brand New

Our house was under construction. The driveway was poured brand new — never driven on, not even once. Our builder strictly prohibited any subcontractors from driving or parking on it.

Monday, July 26

Kinnan Started Work — And Immediately Destroys Our Driveway

Kinnan Scaping arrived. Our builder had explicitly told them not to drive on the new driveway. They ignored this completely, driving a fully loaded CAT skid loader back and forth across the concrete. The driveway had never been touched by a single vehicle before that day. They destroyed it in hours.

The Aftermath

Cracks in 26 Panels, Pitting Everywhere

We found cracks on 26 concrete panels, plus pitting throughout the entire driveway and a chip on our rear patio. The CAT skid loader had done more damage in one afternoon than years of normal use would cause. And Kinnan Scaping? They refused to take responsibility for any of it.

The Lawsuit

They Sued Us — And Won

Rather than fix what they broke, Kinnan Scaping sued us for the balance — $6,443.40. I represented myself against their lawyer and lost. We were forced to pay every dollar. Kinnan walked away with our money. We walked away with a destroyed driveway.
